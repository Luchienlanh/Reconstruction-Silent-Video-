Danh sach loi co the xay ra o phan thi giac
-------------------------------------------

1. Loi crop mieng khong dung vung moi
- Crop qua rong, qua hep, lech len mui/cam, mat mot phan moi.
- Box nhay theo frame lam model hoc chuyen dong gia.
- Training dung crop 96x96 tu mieng, inference dung video full face hoac crop khac scale.
- Video test 112x112 co the da la crop san, neu lai detect/crop lan nua thi mieng bi scale khac voi train.
- Can test: luu grid crop moi inference, so sanh voi cache train.
- Fix: them debug save crops, smooth crop box, dung cung mot preprocess cho train va inference, neu input la crop san thi cho phep --no-mediapipe hoac --input-is-mouth-crop.

2. Loi landmark khong on dinh
- MediaPipe detect dung ty le cao nhung toa do bi jitter.
- Landmarks bi noi suy qua nhieu khi mat detection.
- 40 diem moi chua du thong tin rang/luoi/ham.
- Landmark d1/d2 qua nho sau normalize, model khong thay motion ro.
- Can test: plot mouth_open, mouth_width, landmark speed theo frame.
- Fix: smooth landmarks, them mouth aperture/speed/area vao input, chuan hoa scale theo mouth width thay vi full crop, tang trong so landmark branch.

3. Loi mouth motion qua yeu
- Nguoi noi it mo mieng, motion signal thap.
- Dataset co nhieu frame doc tin tuc, moi chuyen dong nho, model hoc trung binh.
- Video test co mouth motion khac distribution train.
- Can test: correlation giua mouth_open/speed va target energy.
- Fix: filter clip co motion thap, them curriculum tu clip mouth motion ro, them loss energy theo mouth_open.

4. Loi temporal alignment video-audio
- Audio va video bi lech 1-10 frame.
- mel_times tinh sai do fps/hop_length.
- Window lay t0/t1 chua khop voi center frame cua audio.
- Random crop video nhung mel crop chua du chinh xac.
- Inference 25 fps nhung train chu yeu 30 fps, time alignment khac.
- Can test: quet shift tu -10 den +10 mel frames, tim shift co loss tot nhat.
- Fix: shift-tolerant loss trong warmup, auto-sync bang AV offset, luu fps chuan va resample video ve fps train.

5. Loi visual encoder khong hoc duoc
- R2+1D visual tower qua nang so voi data/epoch, chua hoi tu.
- Train moi 2 epoch nen model chua hoc visual conditioning.
- Batch lon 30 + window it co the lam gradient trung binh hoa.
- Visual branch lr thap hon decoder, decoder hoc mel_mean truoc.
- Can test: zero_video/zero_landmarks/mismatch_sample ablation; neu loss khong tang, model bo qua visual.
- Fix: pretrain landmark-only, tang landmark/visual lr, freeze decoder mean branch, train 30-80 epoch, them contrastive/mismatch loss.

6. Loi fusion video-landmark
- Fusion cong residual lam global token lan at temporal token.
- Landmark token chi 1 token/frame, visual co nhieu token/frame, attention co the uu tien global/mean.
- Global token dua vao decoder co the keo output ve average.
- Can test: chay visual_only, landmark_only, gated fusion; do ablation delta.
- Fix: landmark_first/gated fusion manh hon, bo hoac giam global_token trong decoder, them per-frame conditioning vao decoder.

7. Loi decoder sinh theo time thay vi theo visual
- time_direct/time_conditioned branch co the sinh mel mau chung theo thoi gian.
- Neu branch time-only manh, output nghe nhu average prosody.
- Can test: disable_time_direct, freeze time branch, zero visual/landmark xem output co doi khong.
- Fix: disable time_direct mac dinh, phat loss neu zero/mismatch input cho output gan normal, tang dependence vao frame_memory.

8. Loi window training/inference
- Train window 30 frame, inference overlap-add nhung mel_idx co the lech.
- Hann blend lam trung binh hoa qua nhieu giua window.
- Window qua ngan khong du ngu canh am vi/phoneme.
- Window qua dai lam model quay ve mean.
- Can test: window_frames 30/45/60, hop 5/10/15, so sanh delta_ratio.
- Fix: overlap-add dung mel_idx chinh xac, them context frame ngoai window, train multi-scale windows.

9. Loi mask/padding
- mel_mask/video_mask sai lam loss tinh ca padding zero.
- mouth_valid_mask khong duoc dung trong model.
- Padding video/landmark co gia tri zero lam model hoc silence/mean.
- Can test: assert mask length, visualize valid frames, check loss chi tren mel_mask.
- Fix: mask visual tokens, mask landmarks invalid, bo padded tokens khoi pooling/global.

10. Loi normalization cua hinh anh

- Compression artifact cua mp4 lam crop mo.

Test can lam de tim dung nguyen nhan
------------------------------------

1. Mean-collapse test
- Tinh normalized L1(pred, mel_mean) va normalized L1(target, mel_mean).
- Neu pred_vs_mean / target_vs_mean < 0.3 thi model collapse.
- Hien tai score = 0.0606, rat xau.

2. Delta/energy test
- delta_ratio = mean_abs_delta(pred) / mean_abs_delta(target).
- energy_ratio = std(logsumexp(pred)) / std(logsumexp(target)).
- Muc tieu: delta_ratio > 0.6, energy_ratio > 0.6.
- Hien tai delta_ratio = 0.2218.

3. Visual ablation test
- Normal input.
- Zero video.
- Zero landmarks.
- Zero both.
- Reverse time.
- Mismatch sample.
- Neu loss/pred khong doi nhieu thi model khong dung thi giac.

4. Landmark quality test
- valid_ratio phai cao.
- mouth_open/speed phai co dao dong.
- Neu valid_ratio cao ma model van collapse thi loi khong phai detect fail don gian.

5. Alignment shift test
- So sanh loss voi target shift -10..+10 mel frames.
- Neu shift khac 0 tot hon nhieu thi can sua sync.

6. Cache vs raw video test
- Lay mot sample cache co target audio.
- Chay inference tu cache va tu video raw/preprocessed.
- Neu cache tot, raw video xau: loi inference preprocess.
- Neu ca hai xau: loi model/training/loss.

7. Train overfit test
- Overfit 1 sample den loss rat thap va wav nghe duoc.
- Neu khong overfit duoc: architecture/loss/preprocess co loi.
- Neu overfit duoc ma full train collapse: loi one-to-many/data/loss.


Ke hoach sua theo thu tu uu tien
--------------------------------

P0 - Xac nhan pipeline va ghi debug
1. Luu crop moi inference thanh grid png.
2. Luu mouth_open, mouth_width, speed, energy target/pred thanh plot.
3. Them script diagnose cho checkpoint: normal/zero/reverse/mismatch.
4. Log pred_vs_mean, delta_ratio, energy_ratio moi epoch.

P1 - Chong collapse trong training
1. Train lai it nhat 30 epoch, khong ket luan voi checkpoint 2 epoch.
2. Tang anti-collapse loss:
   - lambda_variance 0.2-0.4
   - lambda_energy_delta 0.2
   - lambda_delta 0.3
3. Giam full mel L1 neu no keo ve mean:
   - lambda_mel 0.25-0.5
   - lambda_band 0.35-0.5
4. Disable time_direct va phat mismatch:
   - output normal phai khac output zero/mismatch.

P2 - Tang su dung thi giac
1. Landmark-first model hoac landmark-only pretrain.
2. Tang landmark_lr_scale va visual_lr_scale.
3. Bo/giam global_token trong decoder neu no keo ve mean.
4. Them motion scalar vao decoder truc tiep, khong chi vao encoder.

P3 - Sua sync va data
1. Them shift search trong preprocessing de uoc luong A/V offset.
2. Filter clip co VAD/speech-energy thap.
3. Filter clip co mouth motion thap hoac face speaking score thap.
4. Chuan hoa fps ve 25 hoac 30 dong nhat.

P4 - Them semantic target
1. Tao speech units tu audio bang HuBERT/wav2vec2/KMeans.
2. Model predict mel + unit logits.
3. Loss = mel envelope + energy + delta + unit CE.
4. Sau do dung vocoder/unit-to-speech de tao voice chung.

P5 - Vocoder
1. Cai HiFi-GAN/SpeechBrain hoac vocoder hop voi mel format.
2. Dam bao mel extractor train = mel extractor vocoder.
3. Chi danh gia audio sau khi pred mel co delta_ratio/energy_ratio dat nguong.
